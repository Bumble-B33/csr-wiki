Create and Customize Miniature Armies

Craft tiny clay soldiers in various colors and watch them come to life! Equip them with weapons, armor, and special items to enhance their abilities.

Dynamic Combat

Pit your armies against each other in strategic battles. Arm your soldiers with sticks for melee attacks, give them mounts, or even equip them with special items to make them unstoppable

Easy Item Expansion via Datapacks

The standout feature of CSR is its compatibility with datapacks, allowing you to easily add new items and gear. With who you can expand the clay soldiers' arsenal with custom weapons, armor, and even new abilities.
